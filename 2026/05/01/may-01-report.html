4. Facebook Ads — Noble's Way "Winter River"

Facebook Ads

Three Ad Text Variations — Engineered White Oak at $1.99/SF

Created three unique Facebook image ad variations promoting the Noble's Way "Winter River" — a 7.25" wirebrushed, 1st Quality Engineered White Oak available at $1.99/SF. Each ad targets a different buyer mindset while highlighting the same core product specs and 24,000+ SF in-stock availability.

Ad 1 — Price Shock
Leads with the $1.99/SF price point up front to stop the scroll. Emphasizes that this is 1st Quality — not a second or mill defect — and lists key specs (7.25" wide, White Oak, wirebrushed, 3/8" engineered) to build credibility fast.
Headline: $1.99/SF | 1st Quality Engineered White Oak · CTA: Shop Now
Ad 2 — Spec-Forward / Educated Buyer
Targets buyers who have already been pricing engineered White Oak and understand the significance of $1.99/SF. Presents a detailed spec sheet (species, width, thickness, surface, grade, price) and positions the product for modern farmhouse and Scandinavian-minimal interiors.
Headline: 7.25" Wirebrushed White Oak — $1.99/SF While It Lasts · CTA: Shop This Deal
Ad 3 — Lifestyle + Urgency
Opens by calling out the $6–8/SF price shoppers are seeing everywhere else, then positions Winter River as the trending look dominating Pinterest boards and new builds — available for a fraction of the cost. Closes with urgency: once this lot sells out, it doesn't restock at this price.
