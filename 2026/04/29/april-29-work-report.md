## 1. New Blog Post Published

**Title:** *What Is Hybrid Resilient Flooring?*

A new educational blog post covering hybrid resilient composite (HRC) flooring — what it is, its key benefits such as scratch resistance, PVC-free wood-resin core construction, and easy click-lock installation. The post is designed to position RCF as an authority on emerging 2026 flooring trends and help customers understand this low-maintenance alternative.
