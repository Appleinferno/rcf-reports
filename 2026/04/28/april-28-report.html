5. Google Ads — Retargeting Display Ad

Ad

HydroGuard — "Only 1,000 Sq Ft Left"

Created a new Google Ads retargeting display ad for the HydroGuard floor, capitalizing on the scarcity angle — only ~1,000 sq ft of inventory remaining. The ad uses AI-generated room scene imagery to showcase the hardwood in a kitchen/open floor plan setting and is designed to re-engage visitors who previously browsed the product.

  • Product: HydroGuard Hardwood
  • Angle: Low inventory urgency — "Only 1,000 sq ft left"
  • Ad Type: Google Display retargeting
  • Images: 15 AI-generated room scenes selected
